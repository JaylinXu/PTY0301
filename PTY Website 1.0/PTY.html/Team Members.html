Violet

We call her Captain/Leader Violet because she is seen as the most responsible and accountable member. She provides the rehearsal space and is responsible for correcting and adjusting many of the dance details in the rehearsal. She is a pleasant presence on the team!

Gwyneth

Gwyneth is a girl filled with surprises. She has a chic style, but you will find she has an outgoing nature after talking to her. She leads an adventurous life, has been to many beautiful places and has eaten many delicious foods, which she would enthusiastically share and recommend to the rest of us.

Lanting

Lanting is a very popular presence on our team, and everyone wants to be friends with her. Her popularity not only comes from her attractive physique but also because of her fun and easygoing personality. Lanting demonstrated the fastest improvement in dancing, and she has excellent body control, making her moves look very powerful.

Roxanne

Rox has a strong charisma and always has a lingering fragrance around her. She also has a contrasting personality, talking less at first and seeming to have some sense of aloofness that is not to be messed with. But when you get to know her, you will find her funny.

Cecily

Cecily is the ACE within the team because she is a powerful dancer with seven years of dancing experience. She has a gentle personality and enough patience to teach the members who can't dance. We call her the dance teacher.

Jaylin

Jaylin has shown organizational skills within the team and can often bring everyone together. Jaylin enjoys music, taking pictures and editing videos. She is responsible for many of the teaser and post-editing videos.

Eliwa

Eliwa is the happy virus on the team, with very long hair and a cute hamster look. She always brings us a lot of laughter with her jokes. Her dancing skill is excellent, to the extent that she often surprises all the members!

Liz
